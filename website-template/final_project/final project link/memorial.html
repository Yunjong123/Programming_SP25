In Memory of Those Who Operated in the Shadows

Dr. Elias Vance (1762–1859)

Cognitive manipulation theorist and originator of the Directive Prism concept. Opposed its deployment, insisting on ethical limitations. Passed before Phase I began. His archived notes became the foundation of the 1994 revival.

“I don't believe in control. I believe in understanding.”

Ambassador Nyela Omari (1896–1974)

A voice for peace long before official diplomacy reached certain regions. Secretly organized ceasefires during the late 1950s with Eclipse Dom logistics. Died under mysterious circumstances during a mission in Equatorial Africa.

“A silent voice in a loud war makes the difference.”

Dr. Masaki Ito (1910–1976)

Virologist and pioneer of Project Paragon. Discovered replication inhibitors later used in vaccine research. His sudden death during internal hearings raised speculation.

“I gave them a cure. They feared what it would reveal.”
